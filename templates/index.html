What this tool does

This tool allows you to filter tactics from the Lichess database by pieces used by you and your opponent. This is most useful for looking at different endgame tactics (e.g., rook and pawn vs. bishop and pawn endgames).

How to Use

  1. Select pieces for you and your opponent to have. Note: The more pieces you select, the more puzzles you will get returned which may make the puzzles page load more slowly.
  2. Choose the difficulty range (in Lichess puzzle rating) for your search. If no rating is chosen, all puzzles fitting the specified piece combination will be selected.
  3. Click Submit.
